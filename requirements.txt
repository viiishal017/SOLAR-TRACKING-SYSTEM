Solar Tracking System Using Arduino

This repository hosts the source code and project documentation for a dual-axis solar tracking system implemented using an Arduino. The project's primary goal is to maximize the efficiency of solar panels by continuously aligning them with the sun's position throughout the day.

Hardware Components

    Arduino board (e.g., Arduino Uno or Arduino Mega)

    Light sensors (LDRs or photodiodes)

    Mounting structure

Benefits and Applications

    Increased solar panel efficiency, leading to more electricity generation.
    Suitable for off-grid power systems, remote sensing, agriculture, and various renewable energy projects.

Challenges

    Mechanical and electrical challenges associated with building and maintaining a dual-axis solar tracker.
    Weather conditions and environmental factors that may affect the tracking system's accuracy.
